# Dr. Alan V. Safdi, MD, FACG
## Comprehensive Profile for MD Health Marketing Plan

---

## Executive Summary

Dr. Alan V. Safdi is a world-renowned gastroenterologist, entrepreneur, researcher, and wellness advocate with over 47 years of medical experience. He is the **CEO of MD Health** and serves as a key figure in the health/nutrition bar venture. His extensive credentials, research background, and passion for preventative medicine make him a powerful authority figure for marketing health-focused nutrition products.
